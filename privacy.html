Privacy Policy

Plain-English privacy information for South London Rapid Repairs.

What we collect

When you contact us, we collect information you provide such as your name, phone number, postcode and a short description of the issue.

Why we collect it

To respond to your enquiry, match you with an appropriate independent tradesperson, and help coordinate an emergency response.

Who we share it with

We share your enquiry details with relevant independent roofers/carpenters for the purpose of contacting you and providing a quote.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry records only as long as reasonably necessary for operations and record-keeping.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us via the phone/WhatsApp details shown on this website.
